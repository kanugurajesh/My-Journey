How I stay commited to learning

Stoicism: Understand the principles of Stoicism: Familiarize yourself with the teachings of Stoic philosophers like Epictetus, Seneca, and Marcus Aurelius. Learn about concepts such as accepting what is beyond your control, focusing on the present moment, and developing resilience in the face of adversity.

Practice self-reflection: Regularly reflect on your thoughts, emotions, and reactions to events. Stoicism emphasizes self-awareness and self-improvement, so introspection can help you understand and regulate your responses. Maintain perspective: Train yourself to differentiate between what is within your control and what is not. Focus your energy on the things you can influence and accept the things you cannot change.
